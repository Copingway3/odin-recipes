Potato salad

A traditional creamy potato salad using eggs, celery and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1-2 stalks of chopped celery
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 60 grams (1/4 cups) Mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • A pinch of salt to taste and to salt the water
  • A pinch ground black pepper to taste
    •

    Steps

    1. Bring a large pot of water to boil and salt it liberally. Cook potatoes until fork tender but still firm. This will take approximately 15 minutes. While potatoes are cooking, start boiling water for the eggs. Once the water is boiling, place eggs for 7 minutes to cook.
    2. Prepare the other ingredients while the eggs and the potatoes are cooking.
    3. Drain the potatoes and peel. Place eggs in cold water to stop the cooking process and peel under running water. Cut the potatoes and the eggs and set aside.
    4. Combine the ingredients and mix well. Refrigerate until the salad is cool.
    5. Serve
